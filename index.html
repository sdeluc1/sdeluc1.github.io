About me

I'm a web developer experienced in Ruby, Rails, Javascript, ReactJS, and Redux, but it doesn't end there. The pursuit of knowledge is my driving force, and I will continue to take pleasure in learning new technologies with passion and intensity.

I remember getting my first computer as a major milestone in my life. It was a Packard Bell with a 100mhz processor and a generous 790MB hard drive. I explored every inch of that machine, inside and out, and my life changed forever when I made that first connection to America Online. Many years later, I now have the ability and the desire to create these experiences for others and I want nothing more than to give people this feeling and become connected in previously unimaginable ways.
